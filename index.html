About Idle Science

Your go-to destination for exploring the untapped potential of science in achieving a more equitable and sustainable world.

In an era marked by global challenges like climate change and disparities, it's crucial to leverage the insights of research and expertise for societal and environmental improvement. Our website advocates for the active incorporation of such insights into policy-making and collective efforts.

We offer in-depth articles examining why these insights often remain untapped in achieving sustainability and equity. By highlighting successful examples and innovative strategies, we underscore the transformative power of Cooperative Game Theory as an agent for change. Our platform invites researchers, policymakers, activists, and the public to share ideas and work towards an equitable and sustainable future.
